Introduction of the Movie Data¶

I have selected TMDb Movie dataset for completing this project. This dataset has more than 10k movies from 1965 to 2015. It consists the budget, revenue, popularity, rating, release_year, runtime, cast, director and other movie related variables.

Assumptions¶

  1. I am assuming vote_average as the ratings of the movies.
  2. All the budget and revenue related variables have same unit. Here we assume it is USD.
  3. The revenue_adj and budget_adj are considered here in calculating profits because it accounts for inflation as well.

Questions to Answer¶

  1. The average runtime of the movies and distribution of runtime
  2. The Nature of relationship between Budget vs Profit, Budget vs Ratings & Profit vs Ratings
  3. The movie with highest profit and biggest loss from the data.
  4. Check the ratings of the top 10 commercially most successful movies.
  5. Count of movies with positive profits over the years and the average profits by years

Limitations¶

We see there are a few limitatations to the dataset. Although the total entry was more than 10k movies, but for most of the part we could only perform the analysis on only 3.5k movies due to more than 60% of missing values. Also, there are outliers as well through out the whole data. Keeping these aside the conclusions are below from the analysis we have done.

Conclusions¶

Finishing the anlysis of the movie database for movies released between 1960 and 2015:

  1. The average runtime of the movies is 102 minutes
  2. The scatter plots were not enough to establish any relationship between the ratings of the movies with the profit/budget of the movies.
  3. Stars Wars is the most profitable movie and The Warrior's Way being the least profitable of all. More number of movies made profits than the number of movies that made loss.
  4. The average rating of the top 10 commercially most succesful movies is 7.1
  5. In terms of number of profitable movies, the movie industry has grown as a whole but the average profit is decreasing over the years.
